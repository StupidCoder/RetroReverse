Sonic the Hedgehog (Game Gear) — level viewer

drag to pan · scroll to zoom

The map is an actual tilemap rebuilt from the cartridge — each 32×32 block is baked from the zone's tile atlas. Collision shapes overlays each block's surface height-profile (red) and tints non-solid blocks blue (where Sonic falls through). Objects marks enemies, items and Sonic's spawn.
